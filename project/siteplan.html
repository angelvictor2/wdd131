Calzado River

Site Purpose

The purpose of the Calzado River website is to sell comfortable casual women’s shoes in Guatemala. The site will showcase a variety of footwear designed to provide comfort, style, and quality for women of all ages. It will allow customers to view products, buy shoes online, learn about the brand, and easily contact the business for purchases and inquiries.

Scenarios

  • What is the most affordable price for women’s shoes available on the website?
  • Is there a direct customer service contact option to get assistance or place an order?

Color Schema

The color schema for the Calzado River website was selected to reflect a feminine, casual, and elegant style. Soft and neutral tones are combined with darker accents to create a welcoming and modern appearance.

  • Navy Blue (#1f3a5f): Used for headings, navigation elements, and accents.
  • Soft Beige (#f5f0eb): Used as the main background color of the site.
  • Dark Gray (#333333): Used for paragraph text to ensure readability.
  • Soft Pink (#d9a5b3): Used for buttons and decorative highlights.

Typography

The typography selected for the Calzado River website focuses on readability, elegance, and a feminine casual style. Two font families from Google Fonts are used to create visual hierarchy and consistency across the site.

  • Playfair Display: Used for main headings and section titles to convey elegance and a refined brand identity.
  • Montserrat: Used for body text, navigation, buttons, and general content to ensure clarity and easy readability.
